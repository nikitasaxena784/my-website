Building a Computer:


One of my hardest non-programming endeavors was definitely building this computer from scratch! I made this computer in the summer of 2018 using store bought parts. Some parts include a MSI motherboard, an AMD Ryzen Cpu with 9 core processors, 16gb of ram, a Nvidia GeForce GTX 1050 GPU, and much more. I now use this computer to work on machine learning and computer Science projects. Additionally, from time to time I would also play video games!

Volunteering:


One way I love giving back to the community is by volunteering! I volunteer at a place in downtown San Jose known as the Tech Interactive, Formly known as the Tech Museum. The Tech Interactive is an exhibition of new futuristic technological advancements made by engineers. My duty there is to help lead other volunteers and aid in managing the technology and machines by displaying and repairing them if necessary. This has sharpened my communicative skills and helped me with understanding the hardware in futuristic machines.
